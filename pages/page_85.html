why-bcoe

Top 5 Reasons to Attend BCOE

 

1. Join a Community of Engineers

Be part of a collaborative and supportive environment where you’ll connect with like-minded peers, dedicated faculty, and staff—all focused on innovation and impact in engineering.

2. Engage in Exciting Research

More than half of BCOE undergraduate students participate in research. Gain hands-on experience working on real-world challenges across a wide range of engineering fields.

3. Access State-of-the-Art Facilities

Explore cutting-edge labs, modern maker spaces, and a full-scale machine shop—giving you the tools and technology to bring your ideas to life.

4. Learn from Renowned Faculty

Work alongside over 140 full-time faculty who are leaders in their fields and committed to teaching, mentorship, and student success.

5. Gain Career Opportunities

From internships to industry connections, BCOE prepares you for what’s next—helping you build the skills, experience, and network needed for a successful engineering career.
