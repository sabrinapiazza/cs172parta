Financial Aid

Applying for Aid

Pay for school with scholarships and grants (FREE money), and graduate with less debt. Apply for as many financial aid awards as you’d like — from UCR and all sorts of outside agencies, businesses, and non-profits. You can earn monies from your admitting UCR college or school, as well as for academic achievement, leadership, athletics, and art.

Dining

Dine on BBQ, wok-inspired dishes, fresh poke bowls, taqueria treats, crispy fries, fresh build-it-yourself sandwiches, and all the coffee and tea you can wish for. We also have a great selection of vegetarian, vegan, halal, and gluten-free options all over campus. For those of you with a residential dining plan, you’re going to love the convenient, buffet-style dining experience.

Health, Well-being & Safety (HWS)

UCR offers nine departments dedicated to listening, supporting, and providing students with helpful resources and services for your overall safety and well-being. These include:

  • Basic Needs – Assistance for food insecurity, housing displacement/homelessness, or financial crisis.
  • Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) – Free and confidential clinical services.
  • Student Disability Resource Center (SDRC) – Free, voluntary, and confidential services for students with permanent or temporary disabilities.
  • Student Health Services (SHS) – On-campus primary care, psychiatric services, pharmacy services, and referrals.

Explore HWS Services

Housing Services

Choose from traditional Residence Halls or Family Housing if you’ve got a family of your own. Enjoy a wide range of amenities and ways to get involved.

  • 1,500+ programs and activities
  • Access-controlled and surveilled buildings
  • Live-in staff and 24-hour assistance
  • Tutoring workshops and study groups
  • Computer, game, TV, and study lounges
  • Fitness centers and pools
  • Dining plans
  • Employment opportunities

Discover Housing

Safety

Safety is a top priority at UCR. We have a 24/7, on-campus Police & Campus Safety, an emergency notification system that sends text alerts, a no-tolerance policy against sexual violence and secure residence halls with video surveillance, restricted card access and live-in staff. At night, our Highlander Safety Team patrol halls and parking lots, and Campus Safety Escorts accompany students across campus.

Academic Resource Center (ARC)

When you need academic help, UCR has you covered! Get answers from friendly faculty, engage with study groups, and tap into the free services available at the Academic Resource Center (ARC). You’ll find FREE tutoring, Supplemental Instruction, writing support, peer mentoring, and graduate school preparation!

Career Center

Meet with a Career Center specialist for expert advice, resume help, and interview prep. Plus, take advantage of career fairs and workshops to build the skills employers want. Tip: Visit the Career Center early and often to align your classes and experiences with your goals. This will help to fast-track your next steps after graduation, whether you enter the workforce or pursue an advanced degree.

Undergraduate Education (UE)

UE cultivates student success by providing academic support, experiential learning, and research opportunities. Participate in research projects with our renowned faculty. Intern for a government agency or NGO in Sacramento or Washington, D.C. Create and facilitate your own 1-unit course with R'Courses. Develop academic and personal skills to help you ace your classes with support from the Academic Resource Center (ARC).

For Student Parents

UCR supports student parents with a wide range of campus resources and accommodations.

  • Oban Family Housing – Features apartment homes adjacent to UCR and near to the ECS and Riverside’s public and private schools. Family activities included.
  • The Center for Early Childhood Education (ECS) – Offers childcare and kindergarten classes.
  • R'Kids – A student-led program to connect students, faculty, and staff with or expecting children. Activities and entertainment included.
  • UCR Lactation Accommodation Program – Multiple campus locations with privacy, amenities, and comfortable seating.
  • Youth Activities at the Student Recreation Center – Offers sports, activities, games, and educational instruction.
