What is POSSC?

Payroll Online Shared Services Center (POSSC)

The Payroll Online Shared Services Center (POSSC) focuses on streamlining the payroll process to UCPath for four academic colleges/schools on campus: the Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering (BCOE), Graduate Division, and School of Business (SOB). POSSC is a unit within the Central Personnel Services Unit (CPSU), which is housed at BCOE. Our team consists of experts in academic policies and payroll, enhancing the efficiency for personnel transactions.
