First-Year Admissions Requirements

UCR sets high admission standards. Incoming students are selected based on a wide range of criteria, including academic performance and the potential for success. We "admit to the major," not to the university. High-demand majors are highly competitive. To increase the chance of a favorable admission decision, we recommend students add a preferred and an alternate major to their application.

View Requirements

150+ Majors and Minors

Students can choose from majors like Biology, Business, Computer Science, and Psychology. Accelerated five-year BS+MS programs are also available in Engineering, Public Policy, Entomology, and Statistics.

School Codes

College Entrance Examination Board (CEEB) Code
This 6-digit code is used to identify each education institution. It ensures materials like transcripts and test scores are sent to the right place, which is UCR.
CEEB code: 004839

UCR Financial Aid Applications School Codes
Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA): 001316
California Dream Act Application (CADAA): 001316

Transcripts and Test Scores

If your students are admitted, UCR must receive all transcripts and test scores from every institution they attended and/or are attending. Missing documents will result in a hold and an inability to enroll in classes.

Get More Information

Applicant Roster (Slate.org)

Slate.org is a FREE, secure platform that allows you to access UCR applicant information and monitor your students’ admission status. Once registered, you can upload official transcripts and required documents directly to UCR.
