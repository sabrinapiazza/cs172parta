UC Riverside: Majors and Minors

Engage with brilliant faculty and bold research at the University of California, Riverside (UCR). Choose from over 150 majors and minors, and be guided by leaders in your field of interest. You'll be empowered by peer mentors, learning communities, internships, and undergraduate research as early as your first year.

See dates and deadlines:

  • For Transfers

Notations

M = Academic minors are also available for these majors.
T = A transfer selecting major. Specific pre-requisite courses must be completed if you're applying as a transfer student.
BS+MS = An accelerated Bachelor's + Master's Program to save time and money, and boost earning potential.
BA/MPP = A Bachelor's of Arts and Master's in Public Policy program with an integrated five-year plan of study.
